About Us

Untethered Learning is a collaborative group of lifelong learners who believe that the best education happens when curiosity leads the way. We come from diverse backgrounds and share a passion for exploring ideas beyond the conventional classroom.

📚

Self-Directed

We empower every member to set their own learning agenda and dive deep into topics they are passionate about.

🌐

Open & Inclusive

Our doors are open to anyone with a love of learning — regardless of background, age, or experience level.

👥

Community-Driven

Knowledge grows when it is shared. We learn from each other through discussion, collaboration, and mentorship.

What We Do

01

Study Groups

Small, focused groups that gather regularly to explore a shared topic — from history and philosophy to technology and the sciences.

02

Workshops & Talks

Member-led sessions where anyone can share expertise, teach a skill, or lead a conversation on something they care about.

03

Resource Sharing

A curated library of books, articles, podcasts, and courses recommended and reviewed by our members.

04

Projects & Collaboration

Hands-on group projects that put learning into practice and create something meaningful together.
